Recipe Nook Home

A home for all your recipes! Save, share, and plan.

How to use

You can save recipes for your personal use or join/create a group (such as "roommates", "family", etc.). Members of each group can all view and edit each other's recipes. The following is a list of planned features:

  • Convert recipes from websites, photographs.
  • Edit servings and add recipes to a grocery list.
  • Find an approximate price point of the grocery list; compare prices of different lists.
  • Convert recipes from a website into a clean format.
  • Seamlessly apply substitutions to recipes.
